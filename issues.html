Campaign Priorities

A prosecutor's office should be firm, fair, and focused on what keeps Henry County families safe.

Backing Law Enforcement

  • Strengthen collaboration with local officers and investigators.
  • Improve case preparation so serious charges move efficiently.

Protecting Victims

  • Expand victim communication and courtroom support.
  • Prioritize trauma-informed practices that preserve dignity.

Fighting Drug Crime

  • Target high-impact drug trafficking and repeat offenders.
  • Coordinate with treatment partners when accountability and recovery align.

Smart Juvenile Justice

  • Hold youth offenders accountable with clear consequences.
  • Support early-intervention options that reduce repeat harm.

Transparent Leadership

  • Set clear expectations for ethical prosecution.
  • Keep residents informed with regular public updates.
