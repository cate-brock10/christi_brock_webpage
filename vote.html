How To Vote In Henry County

This page summarizes official county and state voting guidance. For the latest official updates, always confirm details with the county election office before you vote.

1. Register To Vote

Indiana voter registration is managed through the state voter portal. You can check your registration status, update your address, and register online if eligible.

Go to Indiana Voters Portal

2. Vote Early In Person

Henry County offers in-office absentee (early voting) at the Election Office and Voter Registration Office in the courthouse annex.

Location listed by county election information: 101 S Main St, Suite 301, New Castle, IN 47362.

County information also lists typical vote center hours on Election Day as 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

3. Vote By Mail (Absentee)

In Indiana, voting by mail requires meeting an absentee eligibility reason. Submit the county absentee application as early as possible and follow ballot return instructions exactly.

Indiana absentee voting rules

4. Vote On Election Day

Bring acceptable photo ID and go to your assigned voting location or county vote center as directed by local election officials.
